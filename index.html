Frequently Asked Questions

What is string art?

String art is a form of art that creates images using straight lines of thread or wire stretched between points. The arrangement of lines forms intricate patterns and beautiful visuals.

How does this tool work?

This tool analyzes your uploaded image, detects edges, and generates a set of lines connecting pins placed around a circle. The result is a digital string art that resembles your original photo.

What images work best?

High-contrast and simple images (such as portraits or icons) produce the clearest results. Busy backgrounds or low-contrast photos may be less recognizable in string form.

How can I customize my string art?

  • Line Color: Change the color of the strings for a personalized look.
  • Line Thickness: Control the width of each string line.
  • Total Lines: More lines give finer details, while fewer lines produce a simpler effect.
  • Pins Count: Adjust the number of pins around the edge. More pins allow more detailed images.

Can I download my artwork?

Yes! Click Download PNG to save your generated string art as a PNG image.

Is my image stored or shared?

No. All image processing is done in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server.

Any tips for best results?

  • Cropping your image to focus on the subject can help.
  • Try different settings: sometimes fewer lines or pins look better for certain images.
  • Use high-contrast, simple backgrounds if possible.
